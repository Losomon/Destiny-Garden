Frequently Asked

Good to Know Before You Visit

What should we bring?
Bring swimwear, towels, sunscreen, and a change of clothes for the kids. You're welcome to bring packed snacks, though our restaurant and bar serve fresh food and drinks throughout the day.
What are the pool rules for children?
Children under 12 must use the dedicated shallow kids' pool, which is supervised by staff. Children under 5 must be accompanied by an adult in the water at all times. Floaties and armbands are available at the gate.
How do I pay? M-Pesa accepted?
Yes! We accept M-Pesa, cash, and bank cards at the gate. For events and birthday parties, a deposit can be paid via M-Pesa Paybill. Contact us for deposit details when booking your event.
Do you host school trips and groups?
Absolutely! We regularly host school trips, church groups, and corporate outings. Group bookings of 20+ get special rates. Call or WhatsApp us in advance to plan your group's visit and we'll take care of the rest.
Is there parking available?
Yes, we have ample free parking within our gated compound. The venue is accessible from Ruiru-Kiambu Road (Kamiti Road), opposite the Tatu City area. Follow signage from the main road.
Can I book for tomorrow or the same day?
For regular family visits, walk-ins are welcome! For birthday parties, school trips, and events, we recommend booking at least 3–7 days in advance to guarantee your spot and preferred setup.

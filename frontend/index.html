Safety First

Your Family's Safety is
Our Priority

We've built a world where kids can be kids — and parents can genuinely relax. Every measure is in place so you can focus entirely on the fun.

01
Certified Lifeguards On Duty

Red Cross-trained lifeguards stationed at all pools every day during operating hours — zero compromise on this.

02
Separated Shallow & Deep Zones

Kids' pool is physically separated, shallow, clearly marked, and supervised. Adults swim safely in the main pool.

03
On-Site First Aid Station

Fully-stocked first aid kit and trained staff ready to assist any situation swiftly.

04
Supervised Adventure Play

All equipment is age-verified and inspected weekly. Staff present at all activity zones throughout the day.

05
Gated, Secure Premises

CCTV throughout, controlled entry points, and uniformed security personnel for complete peace of mind.
